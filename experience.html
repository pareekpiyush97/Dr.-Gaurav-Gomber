The Journey

From medical school to regional trust

  1. 🎓

    Medical Foundation — MBBS & MD (Paediatrics)

    Completed MBBS followed by an MD in Paediatrics — years of demanding hospital training in newborn care, child development and pediatric emergencies. This rigorous foundation shaped a career devoted entirely to one mission: the health of children.

  2. 🔬

    Specialist Depth in Complex Child Health

    Rather than stopping at general pediatrics, he spent years building advanced expertise in three of the hardest domains of child health — Paediatric Endocrinology (growth, hormones & diabetes), Allergy & Immunology (chronic asthma & immunity) and Gastroenterology (digestion, feeding & nutrition). Today, he is the specialist other doctors refer their toughest cases to.

  3. 🏥

    Multi-Center Practice Across Bikaner

    Built one of Bikaner's busiest pediatric practices across three prominent centers — Marwar Hospital, Kothari Medical & Research Institute and Aashirwad Nursing Home. He manages a very high daily OPD footfall, yet reviewers consistently note he gives every parent unhurried attention — reflected in a steady 4.2–4.3★ public rating.

  4. 🌾

    Regional Trust Beyond the City

    Two decades of consistent results earned him trust far beyond the city. Families from Sri Dungargarh and remote villages travel hours for a single consultation — often after months of failed local treatment — for acute illness, critical newborn care and chronic conditions. Many parents describe his precise diagnosis as a "Midas touch."
