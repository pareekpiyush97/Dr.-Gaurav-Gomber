👪 Parent's Quick Guide

Har parent ko ye zaroor pata hona chahiye

⚠️ Doctor ko turant kab dikhayein

  • 🌡️ 3 din se zyada tez bukhaar, ya 3 mahine se chhote bachhe ko koi bhi bukhaar
  • 😮 Saans lene me dikkat, seene ka andar dhasna, ya lagataar khansi
  • 💧 Dehydration — sookha muh, aansu na aana, urine kam hona
  • 😴 Bachha sust ho, uthaye na uthe, ya doodh/khana bilkul chhod de
  • 🤒 Daure (fits), lagataar ulti, ya bukhaar ke saath laal chakatte

💚 Healthy Child Checklist

  • 💉 Vaccination card time pe update rakhein — koi dose miss na ho
  • 🍼 6 mahine tak sirf maa ka doodh, phir sahi poshan ke saath upari aahar
  • 📏 Saal me kam se kam ek baar height–weight growth checkup
  • 💤 Screen time kam, khel-kood aur neend poori (10–12 ghante, umar ke hisab se)
  • 🧼 Haath dhona sikhayein — zyada-tar infections aise hi rukte hain

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Parents ke sabse common sawaal

Appointment kaise book karein?

Upar diye gaye booking form se apna center, date aur 7:00–10:00 AM ke beech ka slot chunein, phir WhatsApp button dabayein — aapki details ke saath message taiyar mil jayega. Ya seedha call karein: 97844 17672.

Pehli visit pe kya saath lana chahiye?

Bachhe ka vaccination card, pichhli reports/prescriptions (agar hain), aur current dawaiyon ki list. Newborn ke liye discharge summary zaroor layein.

Kya Dr. Gomber newborn babies ko dekhte hain?

Haan — critical newborn care unki khaas specialization hai, jisme janm ke samay ki complications, digestive problems aur feeding/lactation issues shamil hain.

Bahar ke sheher/gaon se aa rahe hain — same day consultation possible hai?

Sri Dungargarh aur aas-paas ke kasbon se rozana families aati hain. Aane se pehle WhatsApp par slot request bhej dein taaki intezaar kam ho.

Asthma/allergy ka ilaaj kitna asardaar hai?

Kai parents ka anubhav hai ki saalon se chali aa rahi asthma ki samasya Dr. Gomber ke tailored inhaler treatment se puri tarah control me aa gayi — bachhe ab symptom-free aur active hain.
